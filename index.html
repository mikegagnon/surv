    Net-centric decision support

    The idea for net-centric decision support (NCDS) is to build the best user interfaces to optimize AI-human interaction as part of an effort to make the best decisions as quickly as possible.

    The crux of NCDS is enhancing the OODA loop, the cycle of observing, orienting, deciding, and acting.

    See Michael Gagnon’s paper: Towards Net-Centric Cyber Survivability for Ballistic Missile Defense.
